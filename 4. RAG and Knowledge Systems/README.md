Retrieval-Augmented Generation has become a cornerstone of production AI applications. This chapter explores the architecture, design patterns, and advanced implementations of RAG systems. You’ll cover vector databases, embedding models, retrieval strategies (dense, sparse, hybrid, reranking), chunking techniques, query rewriting, and knowledge graph integration. The chapter also addresses evaluation of RAG systems (faithfulness, relevance, answer correctness), advanced patterns like agentic RAG and corrective RAG, and strategies for mitigating common failure modes—equipping you to design and optimize knowledge-augmented AI systems.
