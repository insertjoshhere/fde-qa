CloudZero - Multi-Pronged Employee Engagement App

Background: 
Employee engagement is strongly influenced by the quality of day-to-day interactions between employees and their managers.

Many organizations struggle with:
- inconsistent coaching
- infrequent recognition
- lack of actionable feedback
- unclear career development
- poor follow-through after 1:1 meetings

These issues contribute to lower engagement, decreased productivity, and employee turnover.

Recent advances in LLMs enable non-engineers and business teams to rapidly build targeted internal applications that solve these challenges.

Your goal is not simply to use AI.

Your goal is to create an AI solution that addresses measurable engagement problems while aligning directly with business objectives.

Business Objective:
Increase employee engagement by improving the quality, consistency, and actionability of manager-employee developmental conversations.

Success Metrics

Solutions should attempt to improve one or more of the following:
- Frequency of meaningful 1:1 conversations
- Quality of coaching
- Employee recognition
- Goal completion
- Career development visibility
- Manager accountability
- Employee satisfaction
- Reduced voluntary turnover risk

Functional Requirements:
Your application must support at least five of the following capabilities.

1. AI 1:1 Meeting Assistant

Generate agendas based on

previous meetings
current projects
goals
employee concerns

Produce:

discussion topics
coaching questions
follow-up items
2. Continuous Feedback

Allow employees and managers to exchange feedback outside annual reviews.

AI should:

categorize feedback
summarize trends
identify recurring themes
3. Recognition Platform

Employees can recognize coworkers.

AI should classify recognition into categories such as

leadership
innovation
teamwork
customer impact

and generate weekly summaries.

4. Career Growth Coach

Employees describe

"I want to become a Staff Engineer."

AI generates

development plan
suggested projects
learning resources
skills gaps

5. Manager Coaching Assistant

After a 1:1,

AI analyzes notes and suggests

questions to ask next time
coaching opportunities
potential concerns
strengths to reinforce

6. Employee Sentiment Detection

Analyze

survey responses
feedback
meeting notes

Identify

burnout
disengagement
frustration
positive momentum

7. Goal Tracking

Track OKRs.

AI identifies

stalled goals
dependencies
accomplishments
blockers

8. Development Timeline

Maintain an employee timeline of

goals
promotions
coaching
recognition
completed learning

9. Smart Action Items

After every conversation,

AI extracts

tasks
owners
due dates

and sends reminders.

10. Executive Dashboard

Display organization-wide analytics such as

coaching frequency
recognition trends
engagement score
manager participation
follow-up completion

without exposing confidential employee information.

support human review
integrate with existing enterprise systems where appropriate
